About The Bedfordshire Free Press

Independent local publishing for Bedfordshire residents who like their news straight, their satire sharp, and their comedy local.

Who we are

The Bedfordshire Free Press is an independent digital publication covering local news, current affairs, satire, and comedy for residents of Bedfordshire — including Bedford, Luton, Dunstable, Leighton Buzzard, Biggleswade, Sandy, Ampthill, and the villages in between.

We launched in 2024 with a simple idea: people who care about their local area deserve a publication that takes the news seriously and absolutely nothing else seriously. We cover planning decisions, transport, health, education, and community stories — then we skewer them in our satire section before illustrating the absurdity in our comedy gallery.

We are not affiliated with any political party, council, or regional media group. We are funded entirely through advertising and reader support.

Editorial policy

Our news coverage aims to be accurate, fair, and sourced. We follow the Independent Press Standards Organisation (IPSO) Editors' Code of Practice as a guiding framework.

Our satire and comedy sections are clearly labelled and entirely fictional. No satire piece is intended to be mistaken for factual reporting. We do not write satire that targets private individuals; our satirical subjects are public bodies, public figures in their public roles, and local institutions.

If you believe we have made a factual error in our news coverage, please contact us at the address below. We will correct errors promptly and transparently.

  • News articles: factual, sourced, bylined
  • Satire: clearly badged, fictional, inspired by real events
  • Comedy: illustrated absurdist humour, entirely invented

Privacy policy

The Bedfordshire Free Press does not collect personal data beyond what is strictly necessary to operate this website. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

This website may use cookies for basic analytics (page view counts) and to remember your preferences. No advertising cookies are placed without your consent. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

If you submit a message via our contact form, we retain that information only for the purpose of responding to your enquiry and delete it within 90 days.

For any data enquiries, please contact us using the details below. This policy was last updated June 2025.
